Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

What are your customer service hours?

Our customer care team is available Monday through Friday from 9am to 6pm EST. You can reach us by phone at (800) 555-BEAU (2328) or by email at hello@glowandbloom.com. Live chat is available Monday-Friday from 10am-5pm EST.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also log into your account on our website to view your order status and tracking details.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Products must be at least 50% full to qualify for a return.

Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?

Yes! All Glow & Bloom products are cruelty-free and never tested on animals. About 85% of our products are vegan (we clearly label which products contain animal-derived ingredients like honey or beeswax).

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship to the United States, Canada, and the UK. We're working to expand to more countries soon. International orders may be subject to customs fees upon delivery.
