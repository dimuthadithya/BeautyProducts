Product Description

Our Rose Quartz Facial Serum is a luxurious treatment that combines the power of natural rose quartz extract with advanced skincare technology. This serum is specially formulated to:

  • Provide intense hydration for up to 72 hours
  • Reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles
  • Improve skin elasticity and firmness
  • Even out skin tone and texture
  • Protect against environmental stressors

The lightweight formula absorbs quickly without leaving a greasy residue, making it perfect for both morning and evening use. Suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Ingredients

Aqua (Water), Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Glycerin, Rose Quartz Extract, Sodium Hyaluronate, Niacinamide, Tocopheryl Acetate (Vitamin E), Panthenol, Allantoin, Xanthan Gum, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Parfum (Natural Fragrance).

Free from: Parabens, Sulfates, Phthalates, Mineral Oil, Synthetic Dyes.

How To Use

  1. Cleanse your face thoroughly and pat dry
  2. Apply a small amount (2-3 drops) of serum to your fingertips
  3. Gently massage onto face and neck using upward motions
  4. Allow serum to absorb for 1-2 minutes before applying moisturizer
  5. Use morning and evening for best results
