About GlamourGlow

Where beauty meets authenticity and science embraces nature

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Glow & Bloom began as a small passion project between two cosmetic chemists who believed beauty products could be both effective and ethically made. What started as handmade batches in a home kitchen has blossomed into a trusted brand available in over 500 stores nationwide.

We've stayed true to our roots - every product is still developed with the same care and attention to detail as those first small batches. Our team has grown to include dermatologists, botanists, and sustainability experts who help us create formulas that deliver visible results while respecting your skin and our planet.

At Glow & Bloom, we're not just selling beauty products - we're cultivating confidence and self-care rituals that make you feel as beautiful on the inside as you look on the outside.

Our Mission & Values

We're committed to creating clean, high-performance beauty products that are as good for your skin as they are for the environment. Our core values guide everything we do:

Clean Formulas

We ban over 1,500 questionable ingredients, far exceeding industry standards. If we wouldn't put it on our skin, we won't put it in our products.

Science-Backed

Our products are developed by cosmetic chemists and dermatologists to deliver real, measurable results you can see and feel.

Sustainability

From responsibly sourced ingredients to eco-friendly packaging, we're constantly innovating to reduce our environmental impact.

Inclusivity

Beauty comes in all colors, ages, and genders. Our products are designed for every skin type and tone.
